Illuminate

Illuminate, an annual event hosted by apree health, is a marketing event spanning multiple days. Speakers from multiple industries speak on healthcare issues in the United States at panels during the event. This event takes several months of planning, and multi-team coordination to happen.

I was responsible for updating the Illuminate event branding to fit within the new apree health branding, and creating PowerPoint templates, posters, wayfinding signage, swag, emails, social, and printed collateral. This involved weekly meeting with other teams to coordinate event planning, tracking multiple work streams, and delegating when possible.
