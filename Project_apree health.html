apree health branding

In 2022, Castlight Health and Vera Whole Health merged and became apree health. The new company is an integrated health network that combines data-driven personalization, a coordinated care model, and aligned incentives. I, in collaboration with two other designers, developed the new brand identity.



Brand Elements

When the new name was announced internally, the logo and main colors had been selected. It was then up to us to determine the rules around how to use these elements in the overall design. For example, how our background elements should be used in relation to photography.








Social Media

With the new brand, all of our social templates needed to be updated and new ones explored.








Office and Miscellaneous Brand Touchpoints

A company's brand doesn't stop at the webiste. Many touchpoints need to be rebranded in a way that is consistent with the brand's identity.








Events

With apree health having been primarily a software company, most of the branding was digital. This presented interesting challenges when designing for an in-person space.
