Castlight Health Social Media Templates

At Castlight Health, I developed extensive social media post templates to be used across multiple social media sites. Using these templates, we grew the social media following by 75%.



Template Breakdown

When I joined Castlight Health, the social media templates were varied, with a wide range of colors, image treatments, and styles. In collaboration with other teams, I developed templates with standard treatments for headshots, logo placement, background colors and patterns, and more.








Series templates

Within the social media templates, I created multiple series for images we needed for regular posts. These took the standards of how headshots, logos, and backgrounds were designed and broke out in their own way to make an identifiable series, while maintaining a consistent, but not repetitive, feed.
